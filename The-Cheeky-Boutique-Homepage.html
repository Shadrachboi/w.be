OUR STORY

From a Wedding Day Idea to a Cheeky Business

Founded by Debra in Tampa, Florida, in 2017, The Cheeky Boutique began with a simple but meaningful idea: creating personalized, flirty bridal panties for her own wedding day.

What started as a personal project quickly blossomed into a business when friends and family began requesting their own personalized underwear for special occasions. Debra realized there was a gap in the market for high-quality, personalized intimate apparel that could make life's special moments even more memorable.

Today, The Cheeky Boutique has grown into a beloved brand serving customers nationwide, with a particular following among military families, bridal parties, and anyone looking to celebrate life's milestones in a uniquely personal way.

"Every piece we create is made with love, attention to detail, and a focus on making your special moments even more memorable."
